History of Informatics

Welcome to the History of Informatics website, designed and put into action by students of Faculty of Informatics, University of Lugano.

The material has been kept simple to cater to the needs of inquisitive audience with just the ability to use a web browser but concurrently it also contains valuable information for the fellow technophiles. This website aims to explain different areas in the history of informatics ranging from NAND gates to Bill Gates and Steve (engineer and programmer) to Steve (Apple's boss for many years).

In the Timeline, we tried to cover almost 2 decades, from 1968 to 1991.

The website is divided into four sections: Hardware, Software, Internet and Games in order to logically split the history into its main elements and also to make it easier for the user to delve into his favorite section.

Please use either the navigation bar above (also available on each page), or the links below to browse the website. If the page you visit has some related material, there will be a link to the content allowing for an easier browsing experience and a more logical structure between the different areas.
